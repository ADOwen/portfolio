Things that make me happy

The GEEtar

I've been known to pull a few strings. I've been playing the guitar since I've been 17. My friends were in a band at time and one of them let me play with his guitar. I thought it was cool so some months later and 100 bucks down, I met my friend the squier strat and started to teach myself. A couple bad garage bands and a few years later I was seasoned guitar warrior. I love to play and sing and I would do this for a living if it were more stable. I haven't learned anything new for awhile so I'll get on that when I get the chance.

Being Active

I like being active, give me those endorphins please. I've explored a lot of different ways to stay active over the course of my life. When I was just a little junior high boy I fell in love with handball, it's pretty much raquet ball on a budget. If you're not from New York and you know what I'm talking about, you get many cool points. Going into highschool I started getting into boxing and MMA. Then I got obssessed with working out with kettlebells. It was hard explaining what those were to people at the time so I'd be the guy who did "Cowbells". Eventually years later my fried introduced me to Rock climbing, well.. Bouldering anyways. In case you haven't noticed a pattern I fell in love with that. I'd also love to run a marathon. If I could do a few that would be better. Running with my music on is theraputic, yea...

Games and Entertainment

Animal documentaries and documentaries in general rule. Like many a child in my generation, I grew up on video games and my cartoons so yea.... I still do that when I can. If you catch me singing disney tunes, I'm sorry. I'm just waiting for my daughter to get old enough to watch the cartoons I like. When that time comes nobody is going to see me for awhile. Until then catch me on my Playstation, how bout dat? Apparently I like to play on the hardest setting until I'm out of time to lose some more.

Friends and Family

Last but not least, it's the support system! Luckily I have been blessed with a lot of good friends since I was a wee lad and a family that cares about me. Like any other family we've had good times and bad. We've all been through the trenches together and holding strong. Therefore I'd like to thank my parents, my wife, my friends, and my daughter or else they'll all disown me. To all the people I keep working hard to be better for, cheers!
