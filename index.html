Why I want to be a programmer...

Nov. 03, 2021

I love to be challenged and I would love to work on a team of like minded people who are passionate about what they do. I get real nerdy about anything I do. I'm curious at heart and I'm always trying to learn something new. I enjoy the feeling I get when I find a creative solutions for a difficult problem. I believe that there is a lot of room to grow in the tech industry and I love that it keeps the mind sharp. Providing well for my family is a great perk, too. Most of all though, I would like to be a programmer because I can do it remote. This way I can squeeze my daughter's cute little cheeks whenever I'd like.
